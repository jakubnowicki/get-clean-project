Codebook


id variables:
	activityID - name of the activity
	subjectID - subject's number

activity variables:
	same as in raw data;
	t/f - time/frequency
	Body/Gravity - body or gravity signals
	Acc/Gyro - source of signal (accelerometr or gyroscope)
	Jerk/- - Jerk signals
	mean()/std() - mean or sandard deviation
	X/Y/Z - axis
